About Me

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic, Omar Nunez learned the importance of appreciating the little things in life. Coming from a community where serving others is our first priority, really helped shape him into the man he is today. These experiences instilled in him the leadership and discipline it takes to become a very successful agent in his market. As a resident of South Jersey for over 12 years, Omar understands and values his clients' trust, and strives to surpass their expectations every day. With his current marketing approach, passion for perfection, and accommodating service, Omar is convinced that we will establish a winning combination along with the resources of his team. You can count on Omar to be passionate, honest, accountable, and patient throughout the process of buying or selling your home. His genuine desire to serve others motivates him to establish long-term relationships with his clients and to make their real estate experience one to remember. Outside of real estate, you can find Omar at the gym, eating at his favorite restaurant, Sakura, or spending quality time with friends and family.
